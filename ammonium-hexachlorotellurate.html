Ammonium hexachlorotellurate

Ammonium hexachlorotellurate is an inorganic chemical compound with the chemical formula [NH4]2[TeCl6].





== Physical properties ==

The compound forms yellow octahedral crystals about 0.1 mm (0.0039 in) diameter, decomposes gradually in air. The compound contains the ammonium cations [NH4]+ and hexachlorotellurate(IV) anions [TeCl6]2−.
