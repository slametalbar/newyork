Miss New York's Outstanding Teen

The Miss New York's Teen competition is a pageant that selects the representative for the US state of New York in Miss America's Teen pageant.

Erica Parks of New York City was crowned Miss New York's Teen 2024 on July 6, 2024, at Paramount Hudson Valley Theater in Peekskill, New York. She will compete for the title of Miss America's Teen 2025.
